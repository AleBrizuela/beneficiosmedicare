PLAN TYPES

Understanding Your Medicare Options

There are several types of Medicare plans. Each has different coverage, costs, and benefits. We help you understand which one fits your needs.

🏥

Medicare Advantage

All-in-one coverage combining Part A, B, and usually D. Often includes prescription drugs and additional benefits.

📋

Medigap Plans

Supplemental insurance that covers costs Original Medicare doesn't pay. Choose from 10 standardized plans.

💊

Part D Prescription

Prescription drug coverage to help pay for medications. Available standalone or bundled with Medicare Advantage.

👥

Special Needs Plans

Tailored coverage for people with specific conditions or circumstances. Available in eligible regions.
